> We are what we repeatedly do. Excellence, then, is not an act, but a habit.

## Habit loop

```mermaid
flowchart LR
  cue(Cue)
  craving(Craving)
  reward(Reward)
  response(Response)
  cue --> craving
  craving --> response
  response --> reward
  reward --> cue
```

- Habit loop
- Make it easier to do the habit
- Focus on the process rather than the goal
  - Quantity => Quality
- Identity <-> Habit feeding loop
- Actionable takeaways
